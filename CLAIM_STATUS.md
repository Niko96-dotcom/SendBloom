- No hardware capture package was supplied for this closeout.
- Reverb, distortion, gate, and pressure comparison grids: `human_needed`.
- Niko blind or level-matched listening verdict: `human_needed`.
- Explicit review of any stronger fidelity wording: `human_needed`.

`hardware-compared` requires completed objective hardware grids. `fidelity-claim-approved` additionally requires Niko's dated listening approval and explicit wording review. Neither status is assigned here.

Public copy must remain generic. Do not claim exact emulation, component-level replication, hardware matching, or approved fidelity unless this file is replaced by a new evidence-backed ADR-V1-17 closeout.
