    6. Exit codes: non-zero when any automated gate failed (including expected contract-driven ctest failures). Human_needed items do not count as automated PASS and do not alone force exit 0.

    Discretion: wrap ENAB/legal as subprocesses (recommended). Do not require network. Do not add new packages.
